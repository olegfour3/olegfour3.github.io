Oculus Go

One of the very easy graphic ways to draw any object.

We took the Oculus Go as a basis.

For a start, we can create a schematic drawing of how our subject is arranged.

In this case, we have no boundaries. We can do absolutely what we want because itâ€™s just training.

We simply draw lines around our subject, creating closed areas. We arrange these areas in layers, as we need.

Next, we color our areas with a gradient fill. And get rid of the boundaries.

As a result, we get a beautiful graphic object, which is very easy to draw if we need to quickly create some kind of sketch.
