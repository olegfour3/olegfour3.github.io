Logo, Header and Menu

Design of community VKontakte or Facebook.

The first thing I decided to do is to create a logo. I used it for avatar image of my community, or, as it is called, profile picture.

Hyperactive panda is my nickname. Therefore, the logo consists of the first letters of my nickname.

Next I created a header or a banner of my group. Yeah, I'm a fan of the glitch effect. That's why the title "HYPERACTIVE PANDA" is decorated with the effect of television noise. Actually, as well as the logo.

I decided that it would be nice to make a menu for conmfortable group navigation and for quick orientation on community sections.

Inside the menu I have links to some sections. Now our community has something like corporate identity. And the group takes the form of an official community. The menu is decorated via wiki markup.
