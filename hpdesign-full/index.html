«Creativity is nothing but the way to solve new problems.»

There is only one salvation from the exhausting effects of an endless project: break your routine and do something pretty. Coming up with something new every day can be as beneficial for the creative mind as fruit and vegetables are for bodily health. Long-term projects consisting of endless stages, replete with revisions made and brainstorming, can very quickly degrade into inedible decisions and meaningless theorizing. Sometimes a large tablespoon of thick, unrefined creativity can be an excellent remedy to get out of a dead stupor. Creating beautiful things in the order of daily training, you are able to create an archive of small simple ideas that may then grow to a full-fledged project. When you create something magnificent, it does not bring pain and gives satisfaction. Water your mind in little joys and let them remind you of those notepad pages that for the first time brought you a happy and led to graphic design.
